## Board-Man changes

Board-Man changes the original clipboard-manager direction into a workflow-oriented macOS clipboard productivity utility.

Major public-facing changes include:

- Board-Man product naming and app identity
- paste count tracking
- clipboard workflow visibility
- menu bar status feedback
- local QA and permission diagnostics
- public documentation for sanitized OSS distribution
- removal of private/local-only assumptions from the public candidate
